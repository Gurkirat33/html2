Understanding Ingredients

Learn about different types of ingredients and their roles in cooking:

Herbs and Spices

Essential flavor enhancers:

  • Fresh Herbs: Basil, Parsley, Cilantro
  • Dried Herbs: Oregano, Thyme, Bay Leaves
  • Common Spices: Cumin, Coriander, Paprika
  • Spice Blends: Curry Powder, Garam Masala

Basic Pantry Items

Keep these staples on hand:

  • Grains: Rice, Pasta, Quinoa
  • Oils: Olive Oil, Vegetable Oil
  • Vinegars: Balsamic, Apple Cider
  • Canned Goods: Tomatoes, Beans

Fresh Produce

Essential fruits and vegetables:

  • Aromatics: Onions, Garlic, Ginger
  • Root Vegetables: Carrots, Potatoes
  • Leafy Greens: Spinach, Lettuce
  • Fresh Fruits: Lemons, Apples

Proteins

Main protein sources:

  • Meats: Chicken, Beef, Pork
  • Seafood: Fish, Shrimp
  • Plant-Based: Tofu, Legumes
  • Eggs and Dairy

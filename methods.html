Basic Cooking Methods

Understanding different cooking methods is essential for becoming a better cook:

Dry-Heat Methods

Cooking without moisture:

  • Baking - Cooking in an oven with dry heat
  • Roasting - Similar to baking but at higher temperatures
  • Grilling - Cooking over direct heat
  • Sautéing - Quick cooking in a pan with little oil

Moist-Heat Methods

Cooking with water or steam:

  • Boiling - Cooking in water at 100°C
  • Steaming - Cooking with steam
  • Poaching - Gentle cooking in barely simmering liquid
  • Braising - Combination of dry and moist heat

Fat-Based Methods

Cooking using oils or fats:

  • Deep Frying - Submerging food in hot oil
  • Pan Frying - Cooking in a shallow layer of oil
  • Stir Frying - Quick cooking in a wok

Combination Methods

Using multiple techniques:

  • Braising - First searing, then slow cooking in liquid
  • Stewing - Similar to braising but with smaller pieces
  • One-pot cooking - Multiple methods in one pot
